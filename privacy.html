Privacy Policy for FOOD PRAGUE

Effective Date: November 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to FOOD PRAGUE. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application. FOOD PRAGUE is designed to help users discover restaurants, cafes, and food establishments in Prague, Czech Republic.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Information You Provide

  • Language Preferences: We store your selected language preference (Czech, English, Ukrainian, or Russian) to provide you with a personalized experience.
  • Filter Preferences: Your selected filters such as district, cuisine type, price range, and ratings are stored locally on your device.
  • Theme Settings: Your choice of light or dark theme is saved locally.

2.2. Automatically Collected Information

  • Location Data: When you grant permission, we access your device's location to show nearby restaurants and display them on the map. This data is not stored on our servers and is only used to enhance your in-app experience.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device information (device model, operating system version) for troubleshooting and improving app performance.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous information about how you interact with the app to improve functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the app's core functionality
  • To personalize your experience based on your preferences
  • To display restaurants and food establishments near your location
  • To improve and optimize the app's performance
  • To troubleshoot technical issues

4. Data Storage and Security

Most of your data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. We do not maintain external servers that store your personal information. Location data obtained from OpenStreetMap via Overpass API is used in real-time and is not stored permanently.

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

5. Third-Party Services

FOOD PRAGUE integrates with the following third-party services:

  • OpenStreetMap & Overpass API: Used to fetch restaurant and food establishment data. Please review OpenStreetMap's privacy policy at https://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy
  • Google Maps: Used for displaying map views. Google's privacy policy applies and can be found at https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • PayPal: If you use PayPal for in-app purchases or premium features, PayPal's privacy policy applies. View it at https://www.paypal.com/privacy

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: You can view all data stored locally by accessing the app settings
  • Deletion: You can clear all app data by uninstalling the application
  • Location Permissions: You can revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings
  • Opt-Out: You can choose not to use location-based features

7. Children's Privacy

FOOD PRAGUE is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
